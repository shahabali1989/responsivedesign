Swat Valley, Pakistan

Swat Valley is a place like no other. With high mountaints, green meadows and clear lates, it is a place demonstrating the extent of nature's beauty. Famously called the "Switzerland of the east", it surely a deserves an even higher merit.

Check out the hills

The hills and valleys of Swat have breathtaking views to offer. Each with its own unique local touch and not far from each other making it possible to absorb a lot within a small distance.

  • Swat Serena Hotels
  • PTDC Motel Saidu Sharif
  • Swat Continental Hotel

The weather

The weather is mostly pleasant and some of us can get away with light clothing. However it is highly recommended that you take extra layers of clothing especially if you plan to go to higher parts of the valley or to the ski resorts where it is quite cold. Always take the following:

  1. Gloves
  2. Jacket
  3. Other accessories to cover your ear
